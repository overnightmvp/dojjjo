Phase 2 of 7

🗣️ Phase 2: Customer Validation

Talk to real humans | 8 hours
+
VALIDATION RULE: Most ideas are shit. Prove yours isn't before wasting time building.
1

Customer Research

⏱️ 4 hours
  • ✓
    Find 10 people who have this exact problem
  • ✓
    Get their names, contacts, and rate problem severity (1-10)
  • ✓
    Organize research data for systematic validation
2

The Validation Questions

⏱️ 4 hours
  • ✓
    Ask: "How are you currently handling [specific problem]?"
  • ✓
    Ask: "How much time/money does this problem cost you monthly?"
  • ✓
    Ask: "What's the worst part about your current solution?"
  • ✓
    Ask: "If I could solve this for $X, would you be interested?"
  • ✓
    Ask: "Would you pay me $X right now for a solution in 7 days?"
  • ✓
    Count results: 6/10 yes to question 4 = proceed
GO/NO-GO: If less than 6/10 said yes to question 4, your price is too high or the problem isn't painful enough. Pivot now.

Customer validation complete. Ready for Phase 3.
